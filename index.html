Onboarding Optimization & Conversion Lift
Tungsten Automation · Product Analyst · 2024
+19.8%
Conversion
$1.2M
ARR impact
❓ Problem
Trial-to-paid conversion was stagnating at ~12%. PMs had no visibility into which onboarding steps caused drop-off — decisions were based on gut feel, not data.
🗄️ Data
Amplitude event logs (45M+ annual events), BigQuery session data, Salesforce trial records, and Stripe payment conversion timestamps.
🔬 Analysis
Built a step-by-step funnel in Amplitude. Identified 3 steps with >40% drop-off. Ran 4 sequential A/B tests (n=12,400 each) with 95% CI and 80% statistical power.
💡 Recommendation
Simplify onboarding from 5 steps to 3. Remove mandatory credit card at signup. Add a time-to-first-value milestone in the first session.
✅ Outcome
+19.8% trial-to-paid conversion within 60 days of launch. $1.2M incremental ARR. Experiment framework adopted as the team standard for all future feature tests.

Customer Segmentation & GTM Strategy
Magna Infotech · Business Analyst · 2020–2021
3
Launches
−60%
Report time
❓ Problem
3 product lines had no visibility into which customer segments drove revenue. Pricing and GTM decisions were based on intuition — across 50K+ customer records.
🗄️ Data
50K+ transaction records, CRM behavioral data, product usage logs, and competitive pricing benchmarks across 4 business units.
🔬 Analysis
RFM segmentation to identify Champions, Loyal, At-Risk, and Churned cohorts. CLV modeling to rank segment profitability. Competitive benchmarking on pricing tiers.
💡 Recommendation
Focus GTM spend on top 2 segments. Adjust mid-tier pricing by +12%. Sunset the lowest-margin SKU. Build SQL reporting pipelines for ongoing segment tracking in Tableau.
✅ Outcome
Contributed to 3 successful product launches. 60% reduction in manual reporting (~12 hrs/week). SQL dashboards adopted by operations and finance teams as daily tools.
