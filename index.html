About

Who I am & how I work

At the intersection of product, analytics, and stakeholders — clarifying the problem, instrumenting the data, and helping teams make confident decisions.

Product and Business Analyst, 3+ years in SaaS. I sit between the data and the decision — figuring out what the numbers actually mean and what to do about it. I work best in cross-functional teams where I can talk to engineers, designers, and PMs in the same week.

Most of my recent work has been in onboarding and experimentation: running A/B tests, building dashboards that show up in leadership reviews, and helping PMs prioritize based on what users are actually doing — not just what they say they want.
